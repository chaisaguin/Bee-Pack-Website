About Us

The organization was founded on August 25, 2024. Bee Pack is a theoretical organization that is established by
Computer Studies College – Information Technology Students of Xavier University - Ateneo de Cagayan. It is a local
food wraps and package located in Cagayan de Oro City, Philippines. The company sells locally produced lines of
alternative and eco-friendly food packaging products for daily use that are made from raw materials namely beeswax
that are found locally in the country.
